## Section 3 — Lead Magnet Builder

### What is a lead magnet?

A lead magnet is a free resource you give people in exchange for their email address.

It's the first step in your funnel:
Post → Lead magnet → Email list → Product sale

### The 5 lead magnet formats that work best

**Format 1: Checklist**
One page. 7–10 steps. Delivers a quick win.
Example: "10-Step Launch Checklist for First-Time Digital Product Creators"

**Format 2: Cheat Sheet**
One page. Key information summarized.
Example: "AI Prompt Cheat Sheet: 15 Prompts for Faster Content Creation"

**Format 3: Mini-Guide**
3–5 pages. Solves one specific problem.
Example: "The Beginner's Guide to Choosing Your First Digital Product Niche"

**Format 4: Template**
Ready-to-use file. Immediate value.
Example: "Free Sales Page Template — Fill in the Blanks and Launch Today"

**Format 5: Quiz or Assessment**
Interactive. Personalized result.
Example: "AI Digital Product Readiness Quiz — Find Your Best Product Type"

### AI Prompt — Lead Magnet Creation

```
I'm creating a free lead magnet for my target audience.

Niche: [YOUR NICHE]
Target audience: [DESCRIBE THEM]
Main problem they have: [SPECIFIC PROBLEM]
My product: [YOUR PRODUCT NAME]

Please create a complete lead magnet outline for a [CHOOSE FORMAT: checklist / cheat sheet / mini-guide].

Include:
- Title (compelling and specific)
- Subtitle
- Introduction (2-3 sentences)
- 7–10 actionable items or sections
- A closing CTA that points to my paid product

Write it in a friendly, beginner-friendly tone.
```

## Section 7 — Affiliate Insert Map

### What is an affiliate insert?

An affiliate insert is a natural mention of a tool or resource inside your product,
with your affiliate link attached.

When buyers click and purchase through your link, you earn a commission.

### How to add affiliate links naturally

**Rule:** Only recommend tools you actually use or believe in.
Forced recommendations destroy trust.

**5 natural insert points:**

1. **Tool mentions in instructions**
"I recommend using [TOOL NAME → your affiliate link] for this step.
It's free to start and works better than most alternatives."

2. **Resources section**
"Additional resources I recommend: [list with affiliate links]"

3. **Appendix / bonus section**
"Appendix: My full tool stack for digital product creators [with affiliate links]"

4. **Templates that reference tools**
"This template is designed to work with [TOOL → affiliate link]"

5. **PS in the PDF**
"P.S. If you want to automate your email sequence,
I use Brevo (free for up to 300 emails/day): [affiliate link]"
