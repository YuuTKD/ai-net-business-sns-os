# First 48 Hours After Launch — Action Plan

## Purpose

A minute-by-minute and hour-by-hour guide for the first 48 hours
after your Gumroad product goes live.

---

## Hour 0 — The Moment You Publish

**Immediately after clicking "Publish" on Gumroad:**

☐ Copy your Gumroad product URL
☐ Replace {{GUMROAD_LINK}} in every file that still has it
☐ Test the purchase flow: buy your own product at $0 (use a $0 test purchase)
   - Confirm the download file is attached
   - Confirm the post-purchase message appears
   - Confirm the file opens and is not corrupted
☐ Send yourself a test email from Brevo Day 0
   - Confirm it arrives within 5 minutes
   - Confirm the {{GUMROAD_LINK}} in the email is correct (live, not placeholder)
   - Confirm no other placeholders remain in the email body

**If any test fails: fix it before posting anywhere.**

## What "Success" Looks Like in 48 Hours

**Minimum viable signal (continue):**
- 30+ Gumroad page views
- 5+ Tally quiz completions
- 2+ email subscribers
- No broken links or purchase errors

**Strong signal (on track):**
- 80+ Gumroad page views
- 15+ quiz completions
- 8+ email subscribers
- 1 sale

**First sale signal:**
You may not get a sale in the first 48 hours.
Most first sales come on Day 4–7 of the launch sequence
when the email sequence has had time to run.

The 48h goal is not a sale — it's a live funnel with no errors
and early signs of traffic (views, quiz completions, subscribers).

## Troubleshooting Quick Reference

| Problem | Most likely cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| 0 Gumroad views | Link not in post | Add link to first comment on LinkedIn post |
| 0 quiz completions | Tally quiz not published | Publish in Tally → Share |
| 0 email subscribers | Brevo form not connected | Check Brevo form → embed → re-test signup |
| Brevo emails not sending | Automation paused | Brevo → Automations → click Activate |
| ZIP file won't open | File corrupted on upload | Re-upload ZIP to Gumroad |
| Gumroad purchase broken | File not attached | Gumroad product → Files → re-upload |
